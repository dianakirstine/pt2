Dans Dans Dans

Year – 2021 | Project – Dans 1 & 2 | Client – Dance school | Programs used – VSC, Adobe Id, Xd, Ps
Project by: Hakan Koyuncu, Mads Selander, Michala Pedersen, Mathilde Lykke, Sebastean Johansen, Diana Koroma

Assignment in pt. 1:

The task was to develop a website for a fictional dance school targeted at a specific user segment, where we appeared as a web agency. Our first assigned dance school was folk dance.

Work process:

I made use of the development method design thinking, which set the framework for the task. We also made use of qualitative interviews, observations and even danced at a folk dance school.

What I learned:

My role was to design and code the social media page. I also aimed my focus on design and Ps to gain more knowledge in the field from someone elske with more experience. I learned to think and work in a new and creative way based on the design thinking process and iterations. I also gained more knowledge on Ps and editing.

Assignment in pt. 2:

The task was to further develop and test a digital website (mobile format first), which was prepared by another group in part 1. Therefore, we were also assigned a new user segment - this time it was ballet.

Work process:

There was particular focus on visualizing iterations in the design process, wireframes and mockups, and usability testing.

What I learned:

My role was to develop the “medarbejdere” page, and I was in charge of merging all the pages together. I learned about user tests and their importance in the design process. There was particular focus on UX and UI. I also learned a lot about coding, merging, and visualising through moodboards, fonts and colors.
