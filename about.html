About me

I am Diana Koroma - a creative person with a passion for art in all forms. I live in Copenhagen and started studying multimedia design in september 2021.

I’ve always known that I wanted to work with something creative and visual. I’m a quick learner who’s always striving to be better and I stay challenging myself. My creativity has no limits and I love to create projects, [visual] designs and art in all forms.

I've primarily expressed my creativity through modeling and being in front of the camera - so getting the chance to work with the process behind these projects is a new and exciting journey, that allows me to apply technical knowledge to creative work.
