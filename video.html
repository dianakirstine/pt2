Video

Year – 2021 | Project – Video | Client – None | Programs used – After Effects, Premiere Pro, Media Encoder, Audition, Ai, Ps
Project by: Cecilie Schaarup, Johanne Marcher, Shi Cheung, Sarah Larsen, Diana Koroma

Assignment:

In this theme, the task was to make a self-selected “How-To” video that shows a process from A-Z. My group chose to make a video showing how to make a simple roasted veggie dish. We had a vision from the start of animated vegetables that came to life to add comedy and creativity.

Work process:

First we prepared the script with additional scene descriptions, story boards and recorded the voice-overs. Various animations were made for the video, and then we started the actual recording. We used two different cameras for wide- and longshots, and realized during the recording that we should have used extra lighting for certain scenes. In addition, it was limited how many could edit - we decided one was to edit on the big screen and the rest of us directed.

What I learned:

My role was to ensure we got a script prepared with the accompanying scene description and storyboards. Besides that, I also appeared as the actor. I gained a lot of new knowledge about production and editing. Also better knowledge of sound, image composition and light, file formats, and animations.
