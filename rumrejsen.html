Rumrejsen

Year – 2021 | Project – Rumrejsen | Client – DNR

Project by: Jessica Wittrup, Alina Shevchenko, Lasse MV, Mathilde Puch, Diana Koroma

Assignment:

Our first task was to develop concepts and digital solutions in relation to the sale of travel in space. The requirements were sketches, eye-catching images in photoshop and a website coded in HTML and CSS with no focus on merging.

Work process

I started with an individual brainstorms and then one with my group, to get an idea for our concept. We continued with negative brainstorming nd brainwalks before we arrived at Space Jam - festival in space. Next, I sketched the merchandise (which I was in charge of) and the construction of the front.

What I learned:

My role was to develop the front page and the merchandise page with additional merch to go with our concept. I learned the basics of HTML and styling with CSS, and gained some knowledge on Photoshop. In addition, I learned more about idea development, which prepared me for Design Thinking.
