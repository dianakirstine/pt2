Call Me Handy

Year – 2021 | Project – Iværksætterkonkurrencen & SoMe & Branding | Client – Optional | Programs used – Visual Studio Code, Photoshop
Project by: Hanna Egholm, Ida Kelberg, Nina Müller, Shi Cheung, Diana Koroma

Assignment in The Entrepreneurial Competition:

The task was to come up with a business idea based on four specific categories - we chose service innovation and developed a concept that creates service, network and security all in a app. We also entered an online Idea Competition where we pitched our concept for judges.

Work process:

We filled in a BMC and mission template that helped describe the business idea and strategy. A prototype of an app was developed in XD, that was used to test our audience to make sure it matched our concept, mission and fictional future users.

What I learned:

My role was project management along with my co-pitcher. I ensured that all missions were carried out and that order and structure were maintained. In addition, I pitched and learned a lot about entrepreneurship, leadership and project management. I learned more in depth about prototypes, user segments and usability testing.

Assignment in SoMe & Branding:

We worked with online marketing of our product from the entrepreneurial competition, and developed a sales platform. We created a prototype of a website (for mobile and desktop) that refers to our prototype of an app created in Adobe Xd during the entrepreneurial competition.

Work process:

Since we already had a website to take as a starting point, the challenge was to make improvements in terms of usability and appearance. In addition, it also had to be re-coded into mobile-first function.

What I learned:

My role was project manager, which meant that I again kept order and structure. I was responsible for coding the front page, and making 2x content for our social media. I gained a lot of new knowledge about social media and marketing. Including content creation, content plans, branding, trends and tendencies etc. I also learned a lot of new stuff about HTML, CSS and this time Javascript from Youtube. This was the first project we used GIT (-kraken), which I also got better knowledge and understanding of.
